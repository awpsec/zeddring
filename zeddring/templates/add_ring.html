How to Find Your Ring's MAC Address

To find your Colmi R02 ring's MAC address:

  1. Make sure your ring is powered on and Bluetooth is enabled on your device
  2. Use a Bluetooth scanner app on your phone or run a Bluetooth scan on your computer
  3. Look for a device named "Colmi R02" or similar
  4. The MAC address will be displayed as a series of hexadecimal numbers separated by colons (e.g., 87:89:99:BC:B4:D5)

If you're having trouble finding your ring, try bringing it closer to your device and ensuring it has sufficient battery.
